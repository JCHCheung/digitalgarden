# Chronic post-surgical pain
defined as:
- pain that started or worsened ==post-surgery==, 
- at the ==site== of surgery or the innervation of involved nerves, 
- that lasts for longer than ==3 months==

Risk factors:
- certain types of surgery
	- breast
	- thoracic
	- limb amputation
