# Atosiban
competitive antagonist for [[pages/Oxytocin\|oxytocin]] receptors

can be prescribed as low dose (~100µg/min) or high dose (~300µg/min) infusions

possible ADR:
- nausea / vomiting
- dizziness
- hot flushes
- tachcardia
- hypotension
- hyperglycaemia
- injection site reactions
